Room 2 Options

Try each one and pick your favorite for the escape room

A: Scotty's Ice Slide
Scotty slides on ice until hitting a wall/rock. Requires thinking several moves ahead. Same maze feel but genuinely challenging.
B: The Wiring Puzzle
Rotate pipe segments to connect the boiler to the thermostat. Click to rotate, build the path. Classic pipe game.
C: Pattern Lock (Simon Says)
Watch a color sequence, repeat it. Each round adds one more. Survive 5 rounds to unlock. Tests memory and focus.
D: The Cipher Board
Wordle-style word guessing with CMU-Q campus words. Green/yellow/gray feedback. 6 attempts max.
E: Lights Out
5x5 grid of freezing AC vents. Click one to toggle it + neighbors. Turn all off to fix the temperature. Classic logic puzzle.
